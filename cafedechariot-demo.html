Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025. At Cafe De' Chariot, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when placing orders via WhatsApp, including your name, phone number, and delivery address. This information is used solely to fulfil your order.

How We Use Your Data

Your contact information is used exclusively for order processing and delivery coordination. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

WhatsApp Communication

When you use our WhatsApp ordering system, your conversation is subject to WhatsApp's privacy policy in addition to ours. We retain order records for accounting purposes only.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand visitor behaviour. No personally identifiable information is collected through these tools.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at cafdechariot@gmail.com or call +92 300 1452900.

Terms of Service

By using the Cafe De' Chariot website and placing orders, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Orders & Payment

All orders placed via WhatsApp are subject to product availability. Prices displayed on this website are current and may be subject to change. Payment is accepted upon delivery or pickup unless otherwise agreed.

Delivery

Delivery is available within the local Rahim Yar Khan area. Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on demand and location. We are not responsible for delays beyond our reasonable control.

Menu Items

Menu items and prices may change periodically. Images shown are for illustrative purposes. Actual product presentation may vary slightly.

Operating Hours

Cafe De' Chariot operates from 06:00 PM to 10:00 PM daily. Orders outside these hours will be processed on the next available day.

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement by contacting us directly via WhatsApp or phone. After this window, cancellations may not be accepted if food preparation has begun.
